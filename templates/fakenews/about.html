About

Scopolamine

The idea is to tackle the issues of fake news and false information, through our platform to help the community. We aim to provide a portal that allows users to check/predict whether a particular news is true of false. Additionally, our fake news detection platform will also provide users with real/true news regarding the topics of news they searched. It is important to spread awareness about the fake news, hence we also aim at providing insights and analysis on fake news. The platform aspires to aware people and help them by providing these services on our portal.
